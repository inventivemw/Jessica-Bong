Title: Half the Sky

----

Year: 2014

----

Text: A redesign of 'Half the Sky,' essays and articles by Nicholas D. Kristof and Sheryl Wudunn. The publication covers investigation into the injustices committed against women within developing nations. Harrowing accounts of sexual slavery, human trafficking, rape, female circumcision and the overall subjugation of women are covered in hopes of championing awareness and civilian action.
