Title: MUMA Exhibition Guide

----

Year: 2014

----

Text: Museum University of Art presented an exhibition entitled 'Concrete,' considering the impact of time upon built and monumental forms. Featuring 15 diverse artists, curated by Geraldine Kirrihi Barlow. The schedule was meticulously hand printed with gold ink onto porous cream stock, paying respect to the monuments shown in the exhibition.
