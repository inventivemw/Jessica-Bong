Title: Ephemera ^2

----

Year: 2014

----

Text: Proposed branding for Exhibition held at the (link: http://www.architecture.com.au/ text: MADA Gallery) at Monash University Caulfield's campus. Exhibition showcased 60 of Final Year Publications dedicated to finalists in the (link: http://www.architecture.com.au/events/state-territory/victorian-chapter/victorian-awards text: Victorian Architecture Awards) held by the (link: http://www.architecture.com.au/ text: Australian Institute of Architects). The premise of the design was to allude to the transience of paper, and the medium graphic designer's use daily in contrast to the more permanent structures created by Architects.
